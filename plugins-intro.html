What Are Claude Code Plugins?

Plugins are specialized AI agents with domain expertise. Think of them as expert consultants who know your institutional standards, frameworks, and best practices - and can apply them instantly to your work.

üí° Real Example

The Education Toolkit plugin knows:

  • Quality Matters (QM) standards for rubric design
  • WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility guidelines
  • Bloom's taxonomy for learning outcome verbs
  • Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles
  • Ivey's 6-phase course development process
  • Evidence-based assessment methodologies (PAIRR, AI Roleplay, Diagnostic Rubrics)

It applies this knowledge automatically - you don't need to explain QM standards every time you ask for a rubric.

How Plugins Differ from Regular Claude Code
